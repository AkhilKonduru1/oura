Oura Ring Analytics Dashboard

Visualize your health data with interactive charts and AI-powered insights

What is this?

This is a free, open-source analytics dashboard for your Oura Ring data. Unlike the official Oura app which requires a monthly subscription for detailed insights, this tool lets you visualize and analyze all your health metrics completely free.

How to Get Your Oura Data

1

Go to Oura on the Web

Visit cloud.ouraring.com and sign in with your Oura account

2

Access Your Settings

Click on your profile icon in the top right, then select "Account Settings"

3

Download Your Data

Scroll to "Data Export" section and click "Download Data Archive". You'll receive a ZIP file with all your CSV files

4

Extract and Upload

Unzip the downloaded file and upload all CSV files using the button below

Note about data processing time

The data export can take a few minutes to generate. Oura will send you an email when it's ready to download.
